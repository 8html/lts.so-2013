LTS >> About

Foshan Shunde Rators Decorating Materials Co.,Ltd. is a professional enterprise specialized in manufacturing and selling such decoration materials as aluminum ceiling,interior and exterior aluminum curtain wall,non-standard ceiling,ceiling accessories. Established in 2007,the company covers an area of 8000㎡ with the workshop reaching more than 6000㎡,with hundred of employees.

Our company has developed into a group which focuses on marketing with the combination of designing,producing,selling and installing based on the rapid development in the several years. As a result of our high quality products and outstanding customer service, we have gained a global sales network including not only hundred of exclusive stores of ceilings in domestic market,but also the foreign markets like Singapore,Vietnam,Russia,Nigeria,America.

Additionally,the company has introduced the most advanced professional processing equipments and computerized numerical hydraulic equipments from Holland in order to guarantee total customer satisfaction.All procedures from raw materials sourcing,engineering design,products inspecting,products packing,installation service are performed under strict control and management according to the ISO9001:2008 Quality Management System.

Insisting on the purpose of "excellent quality,best service",the company is constantly innovating and dedicated to open the innovative concepts of the industry.With excellent raw materials,advanced technology,precise manufacturing,the company is developing green,environmental protective,comfortable,elegant and healthy building materials to adapt to the century developing subject of new green.
