New fashion style in kitchen ceiling

2013-04-18

To meet "the kitchen space integration" of the home improvement trend, more and more integrated ceiling manufacturers will originally isolated from heating, ventilation, lighting, ceiling and other functions of the integration of traditional interior design, injection of more individualized consideration, integrated combination, function integration, so that each component plays a more prominent synergistic benefits, for home furnishing life to create a comfortable environment ideal, as their ultimate goal. Individualized, integrated, intelligent, functional home furnishing design, is the direction of home space design .

The real" top bath" is not a simple electrical plus ceiling, but as a whole and design considerations. They are often very easy disassembly, shift, a good bath top will be more electrical installation in a " special" mounting bracket, it can make the electric appliance moving, disassembling and assembling conveniently.

A true top bath should have three functions: first is the function effect, according to the use of space layout position optimization, through the combined installed electrical function get the biggest play; followed by the decorative effect, designers, the spatial layout should be fully considered, such as wall, water color device installation, mirror front lamp design, through a unified planning, ensure the spatial design in harmony; third is the effect of safety, electrical components should choose high quality brand-name products, should give full consideration to the bathroom and toilet humidity characteristics, choose the overall ceiling, must put the electrical components of the safety in the first place.
