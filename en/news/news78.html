Tips in consumers buying integrated aluminum ceiling plate:

2013-04-18

Rators integrated ceiling device uses a full set of steel structure dressing as free for customers . In addition, all dressings are marked with Rators trademark words, so, you don't have to have any quality concerns, device has completed, you can in the warm family." Enjoy the top beauty".

Some are using wooden keel installation mode, the integrated ceiling brands. While Rators integrated ceiling is the steel keel installation. Then, why should the use of steel keel installation? The reason is very simple -- because the wood can be affected by temperature and humidity effects will be deformed, take a look of your own home wood flooring, doors and other wood products, in summer, wet weather and winter dry weather distortion levels, as known all, the wood between some deformation, some small, the final result is the ceiling appear uneven deformation for a long time . On the contrary, with light steel keel method device, because the ceiling above all data is light steel metal to minimize the deformation, therefore, Jbaili integrated ceiling with light steel keel installation is the most desirable .

So a lot of decoration company adopts an approach of wooden installation, usually works must with the installation of light steel keel in home decoration due to short distance. Because wooden device has the advantages of simple construction, at the same time dressing cost is very cheap, a bundle of wood, but a few money, the entire room is enough dressing interest almost zero. And light steel keel dressing general interest in RMB10 per square meters, including 38 keel, metal rods and other accessories.

In fact, most are wooden keel, some building materials agents quote RMB 15/㎡in order to attract consumers. You will think you have bought something cheap, in fact you suffer, general light steel keel device costs about RMB 20 per square metre, decoration company quotes RMB 50 or more, if someonegives you an offer for RMB 15 per square metre price, we suggest you to be careful. All business is for money, no one is willing to win the repute but lose money. If with such low price, or the process of rough, originally a day to finish the task, if 2 hours has finished, or other way, such as substitution, give you a fake, therefore, strongly recommend to the decoration of the friend, pls do not be penny wise and pound foolish, think first, why the price will be so cheap.
